About

The Art of Slow Making

Kass Kreative was born from a love for hands-on craft and the belief that the things you carry every day should be made with intention. Each tri-fold journal is crafted from 100% full grain Italian vegetable-tanned leather, bound with hand-selected string, and finished with metal eyelets — no two are exactly alike.

Whether you're journaling, sketching, or documenting your life, your journal is meant to evolve with you. The leather softens, deepens, and develops a rich patina over time, making each journal uniquely yours.

No mass production. Every journal is made to order, slowly and intentionally — one at a time.

Shipping

Shipping Notice

We ship worldwide! Please allow up to 14 business days for your journal as it's crafted by hand. Your order will ship once payment is received. Shipping rates vary depending on your country and the weight of the package.

Please note: Import duties and taxes may apply and are the responsibility of the customer.

Policies

Payment

All customers will receive a secure payment link via Stripe once orders are placed through direct message. Orders are only added to the production queue once payment is confirmed.

Refund & Return Policy

All sales are final. Each journal is handmade and produced in limited quantities, making each piece unique. Due to the nature of handmade goods, we do not accept returns, exchanges, or cancellations once an order has been placed.

Please read the product description carefully before purchasing — dimensions, materials, and other details are listed to help ensure you know exactly what you are ordering. If you have any questions prior to ordering, don't hesitate to reach out via DM on Instagram or TikTok @kasskreative.
